About Back Bay

Back Bay was the first distict in the city of Boston. From there the city grew into the urban powerhouse it is today. There is so much to do in Back bay from learning about the history to learning abut the modern advances the city is making.

Transit Information

Back Bay has its own stop on the commuter rail line comming from all around the city and surronding towns. There are also intercity modes of tranportation such as busses and subways that stop in different parts of Back Bay. Besides public transport tation their are also cabs, ubers, and lyfts. Personally the best way to travel is on foot.
